★ About JJBA ★

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure is a Japanese manga series created by Hirohiko Araki. It also has been adapted into an animated series that you can watch on Netflix. JJBA tells the story of the Joestar family, a family whose various members discover they are destined to take down supernatural foes using powers that they possess. The story is split up into unique parts, each following a member of the Joestar family, who typically have names that can be abbreviated to the titular "JoJo". [via Wikipedia]
